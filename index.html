Outreach

I am running the UIUC High School Girls' Astronomy Summer Camp 2023. We work with local high schoolers on a variety of astronomy-related projects, including night observing, spectroscopy, and a coding exercise.

In 2023, I ran the regional and state Solar System B events for Illinois Science Olympiad.
